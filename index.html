About Me

I was born and raised in Edmonds, WA. After living in Southern California for 8 years, I now reside in Tacoma, WA with my husband and two mini austrailian shepards.

In 2007, I started working right out of high school at Merrill Lynch as a Client Associate. After 9 years, I worked my way up to become a Team Private Wealth Associate.

After resigning from my position, I walked across the country of Spain over the span of 45 days.

For the last 2 years I have been a Fire Dispatcher for Pierce County and run my own house cleaning business, Forrest Green Cleaning, LLC.

My goal is to help others through web development!
